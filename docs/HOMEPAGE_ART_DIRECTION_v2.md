# Homepage Art Direction — v2 (Calm Executive)

> **Status:** Approved direction for homepage rebuild  
> **Replaces:** All previous homepage visual experiments (blue/navy template, decorative diagrams, chapter numbering, overlapping panels)

---

## North star

A timeless Hungarian enterprise consultancy — not a React landing page. The visitor should feel **calm confidence**: intelligent people who lead serious transformations.

**Impress through:** composition · typography · hierarchy · spacing · restraint  
**Never through:** effects · decoration · overlapping layers · multiple visual systems fighting

---

## Brand anchor

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Logo | Unchanged asset — visible in header on every scroll position |
| Primary colour | Logo green `#2A6B52` — headlines, rules, links, key numbers |
| Dark accent | Forest green `#1E4D3A` — **max 2 sections** per page (motto + closing) |
| Background | Warm white `#F6F3EE` — default canvas (70%+ of scroll) |
| Secondary surface | Stone `#EBE7E0` — one alternate chapter only |
| Graphite | `#2A2C28` — **avoid on homepage** unless one intentional moment |
| Blue | Not used on homepage |

---

## Homepage scroll rhythm (7 chapters, not 10)

```
Warm white  → Opening statement (typography)
Warm white  → Shackleton quote (editorial)
Stone       → Three paths (team · consulting · training)
Dark green  → Leadership question (motto)
Warm white  → Six reasons (partnership — signature through content)
Warm white  → Evidence (stats, typographic)
Warm white  → EU & pályázatok (trust, official programme mark)
Warm white  → Referencia (case-study tone, minimal)
Warm white  → Mentally (product island — minimal, separate)
Dark green  → Kapcsolat (closing)
```

No navy. No decorative SVG networks. No Roman numerals. No floating panels.

---

## Signature moment

**Chapter V — Hat ok:** Six reasons presented as a **confident editorial sequence** — large green numerals, uppercase titles, generous leading. Reads like an executive memo, not a diagram. The company's own words are the wow.

---

## EU funding

Dedicated trustworthy section — not a sidebar teaser.

- Verbatim pályázat intro from site
- Verbatim EU/ÁROP/TÁMOP experience line from service templates
- Official `illust_2020.svg` programme mark (from company assets)
- Calm two-column editorial layout — no badges, no chaos

---

## Mentally

Separate **product island**: extra whitespace, neutral background, no green band sharing. Feels like a distinct premium tool — minimal copy from backup, single link.

---

## References

One case-study tone block — headline + verbatim „Büszkék vagyunk…” + link. No logo grid on homepage.

---

## Motion

- Optional single fade-in per section (`opacity` only, 500ms)
- No stagger chains, parallax, path-draw, or hover transforms on content
- `prefers-reduced-motion`: no animation

---

## Mobile

- Separate layout decisions (not scaled desktop)
- Single column, full-width type, touch-friendly links
- Stats stack with number left / label right
- EU mark above copy, not beside

---

## Content rule

100% backup wording. No AI marketing. No invented chapter titles.
